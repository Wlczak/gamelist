Make tasks rewarding

Gamelist turns your to-do list into a simple game — attach point rewards to goals, earn points by completing tasks, and spend points in your personal rewards shop.

Get started How it works
Open source — github.com/Wlczak/gamelist
Create tasks
Add tasks and assign point values to each goal.
Earn points
Mark tasks done to collect points instantly.
Redeem rewards
Use points in your rewards shop — add custom items and prices.
Who is this for?
Anyone who wants a little extra motivation — students, hobbyists, people tracking habits or side projects.
